Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Introduction

Harvey Fiser ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you interact with our SMS/text messaging service.

Information We Collect

When you opt in to our SMS messaging service, we collect only the information necessary to send you text messages:

  • Your mobile phone number (provided by you upon opt-in)
  • Your opt-in consent and the date/time of consent
  • Records of messages sent and received (for compliance purposes)

We do not collect any additional personal data through our SMS messaging service. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools in connection with our text messaging program.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number and opt-in information are used solely for the following purposes:

  • To send you informational text messages about events, programs, and updates
  • To process your opt-out requests
  • To respond to HELP requests for customer support
  • To maintain records of consent as required by applicable regulations
No Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, lease, trade, or otherwise share your personal information — including your phone number — with any third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose. Your information will never be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your phone number and opt-in records only for as long as you remain an active subscriber to our messaging service. If you opt out by texting STOP, we will retain a record of your opt-out request to ensure we honor it, but your number will be removed from our active messaging list.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we hold. Our messaging service is operated through Twilio, a leading cloud communications platform that maintains industry-standard security practices including encryption in transit and at rest.

Children's Privacy

Our SMS messaging service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify subscribers via text message. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was most recently revised.

Opt-In & Consent

By providing your mobile phone number and opting in to our messaging service, you expressly consent to receive recurring informational text messages from us. Consent is not a condition of any purchase or service. You may opt in by providing your number through our designated sign-up process.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You can expect to receive approximately 2–4 messages per month, though frequency may increase around events or program announcements. We will not exceed a reasonable number of messages.

Opting Out

You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to the short code. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us. If you want to join again, just sign up as you did the first time and we will start sending SMS messages to you again.
