FERPA & Data Privacy

Institutional compliance requirements for contact data

FERPA Notice: This database may contain educational records protected under the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). All users are responsible for safeguarding personally identifiable information (PII) and must not disclose protected records without proper authorization.

Data Handling

Do not export or share contact data outside authorized Else School personnel. Delete exported files when no longer needed.

Access Security

Sessions auto-expire after 30 minutes of inactivity. Always sign out when leaving your workstation. Do not share login credentials.

Export Policy

All data exports are logged. Exported data must be stored on secured, college-managed devices only.

Incident Reporting

Report any suspected data breach or unauthorized access to the Else School administration immediately.
